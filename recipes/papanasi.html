Papana»ôi Recipe

Description

One of my favorites Romanian desert dishes made with sweet cheese, basically cheese doughnuts

Ingredients

  • Cottage cheese
  • Flour
  • Cream cheese
  • Jam
  • Vanilla sugar
  • Eggs
  • Lemon zest
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Blend the cottage cheese
  2. Dough
  3. Form donuts
  4. Heat oil
  5. Fry
  6. Remove excess oil
  7. Serve
