Gin and Tonic Recipe

Description

A refreshing recipe for a nice summer afternoon

Ingredients

  • Ice cubes
  • Gin
  • Tonic water
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Lime wedge

Steps

  1. Add ice cubes in the glass
  2. Pour gin
  3. Add tonic
  4. Add lime juice
  5. Stir well
  6. Garnish with lime wedge
