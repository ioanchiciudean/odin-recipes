Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Make the cheese mixture.
  3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
